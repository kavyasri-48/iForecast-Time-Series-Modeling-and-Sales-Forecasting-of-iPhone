iForecast: Time-Series Modeling and Sales Forecasting of iPhone

Predicting the future of iPhone sales in India using data analytics, time-series modeling, and machine learning.

iForecast is a data analytics and machine learning project that explores the growth and future trajectory of Apple's iPhone in the Indian smartphone market. Using time-series modeling, historical sales trends, and predictive analytics, this project forecasts iPhone sales and revenue growth through 2036. The platform provides interactive dashboards and visual insights that help understand market performance, product demand, and future opportunities for Apple's iPhone ecosystem in India.
